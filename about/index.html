About

So, what is the 2021 Design Refresh, anyway?

What is this?

The 2021 Design Refresh was a community-centered weekly creative art challenge. It ran online from January to May and was organised by Cindy Xu .

Many creators worked hard to create work according to a theme every week. At the end of each week, a showcase was broadcast live on Twitch for the community to enjoy.

This website hosts the themes and showcases all entries from the #2021DesignRefresh .

Cwown?

“Graphic Design is my Passion.” - Cindy “Tuna” Xu

Cindy (she/her) is a professional clown*, freelance graphic designer, and Twitch streamer.

She goes by the streamer alias “CindryTuna” and the professional freelance alias “FiveClawD”. She frequently streams art on Twitch but also does variety streams including gaming and chatting.

* In behaviour, not in profession.

How does this site work?

This website was written, maintained, and deployed by Thomas “papapastry” Wang (he/him), principal software engineer for Inkling Interactive with graphic design assets by Cindy.

The site was updated semi-automatically from submissions posted in the community Discord server .

Due to the automatic nature of this website, even though we moderate submissions, you may run into a few minor issues. If so, feel free to raise a bug ticket and the team will get on it as soon as possible.

You can also see upcoming changes in GitHub Issues if you’re interested.
